Exponential Moving Avarage


How does the strategy works ?

  • Buy when the 5-period EMA   crosses from below to above the 20-period EMA  , and the price, five, and 20-period EMAs are above the 50 EMA  .
  • For a sell trade, sell when the 5-period EMA   crosses from above to below the 20-period EMA  , and both EMAs and the price are below the 50-period EMA  .
  • Place the initial stop-loss order below the 20-period EMA   (for a buy trade).

Moving Average Convergence Divergence


How does the strategy works ?

  • Trade the MACD   and Signal line   crossovers. Using the trend as the context, when the price is trending higher (MACD HIST   should be above zero line), buy when the MACD   crosses above the Signal line   from below. In a downtrend (MACD HIST   should be below zero line), short sell when the MACD   crosses below the Signal line  .
